Welcome

The biannual conference of the International Society for Research on Emotion brings together researchers from all the disciplines involved in the study of emotion from all over the world to meet and share their work. Come to ISRE 2017 to learn the state-of-the-art in emotion research.

As an emotion researcher—whether from the humanities, the social sciences, the behavioral and brain sciences, or any other domain—ISRE 2017 awaits you. We look forward to seeing you in St. Louis next July!

Hillary Elfenbein (Olin School of Business, Washington University)
on behalf of the ISRE 2017 scientific and organizing teams
