Submit an Abstract

The deadline to submit an abstract is January 10, 2017.

Presenters must be ISRE members at the time of the conference, and may only be the first author on one abstract.

For empirical abstracts, the data must be collected prior to submission. The abstract must describe the goals of the study, the method, a summary of the results, and conclusions.

Authors may submit an abstract for the following types of presentation:

Symposium

Symposia are 1 hour 40 minutes and include 4-5 talks. The recommended time per talk is 20 minutes (15 minutes for the talk, 5 minutes for questions).

Conveners must submit:

  • Symposium title
  • Symposium general abstract (up to 750 words)
  • At least three keywords for the symposium
  • Author information, title, and abstract (up to 400 words) for each talk

Individual Talk

Individual talks will have a 20-minute time limit (15 minutes for the talk, 5 minutes for questions).

Authors must submit:

  • Talk title
  • Author information
  • Talk abstract (up to 400 words)
  • At least three keywords

Poster

Posters will be assigned to dedicated sessions and stay up for a day. They should be sized at approximately A0 (841 x 1189 mm, or 33.1 x 46.8 in).

Authors must submit

  • Poster title
  • Author information
  • Poster abstract (up to 400 words)
  • At least three keywords

To submit an abstract, first create an account at EasyChair. Then visit the submission link.
