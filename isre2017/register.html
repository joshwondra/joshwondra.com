Register

The registration fee for ISRE 2017 includes admission to all program sessions (plenary and parallel sessions, poster sessions, and symposia), the conference package (conference documentation, badge, program booklet, etc.), coffee breaks and all the activities of the social program (e.g., welcome and farewell receptions). The conference dinner and other meals are not included.

The conference dinner will take place on Thursday 27th July at (LOCATION). Attendance tickets (50 USD) can only be bought during the online registration, and only until June 24th.

Online registration is possible until July 5 (early bird registration until May 1). On-site registration will also be possible on July 27 (17:30 - 20:30) and July 28 (8:00-10:30) at the registration desk. Please note that on-site conference registration will require cash payment in US dollars.
