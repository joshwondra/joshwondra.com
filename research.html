I research emotion with an emphasis on empathy—feeling what others feel—and sympathy—feeling concern for others.

I work with my advisor, Phoebe Ellsworth, to connect appraisal theory of emotion to empathy. I study how vicarious emotions are based on the way that we evaluate others' situations on dimensions like agency and certainty.

In my lab we come up with creative ways to evoke firsthand and empathic emotions. We've used a radio show based on This American Life, news stories, and recall of emotional memories.
