I teach courses on social psychology, emotion, and statistics.

Most of my students won't become psychology researchers, and I think that's great! In my teaching I focus on developing students' transferrable skills, research skills, and data analytic skills that will help them achieve their professional goals, whatever those goals might be. The specific course content just gives us something fun to study while we practice those skills.
